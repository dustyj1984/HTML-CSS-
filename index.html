Biography

My life has not followed a typical trajectory looking like a bunch of scribbles on a piece compared to a nice straight line. Some state this is because of a lack of focus, but I push back on that notion.Life is complex, and if one really wants to be successful, they must develop many skills with a bored knowledge base that enables them to understand how the world works. I would dare to say that the scribbles all over the paper display a greater focus than simply following a predetermined path.So, I can summarize my life biographyTM with a simple I live by, "I am not trying to be correct; I am just trying to get the correct answer."
